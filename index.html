About

Dream Body cares of your weight so,
it takes care of the process of calculating your BMI(Body Mass Index)
then decides based on the result. A high BMI can indicate high body fatness,
low BMI can indicate healthy weight or underweight.
BMI screens for weight categories that may lead to health problems,
but it does not diagnose the body fatness or health of an individual.
